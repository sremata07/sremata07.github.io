## Software Engineering, yay or nay

Software engineering was I would say my favorite class for this semester. While I struggled to do every experience on time due to the sheer multitude, I could truthfully say I enjoyed working on what I did this semester. This class taught me a lot about how I myself manage time, what I could expect in the future if I were to follow this sort of pathway in life, and the intricacies that web development beyond making the page look nice.
